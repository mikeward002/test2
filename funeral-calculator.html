Frequently asked questions

Where does this data come from?

We use Google Maps and Places to discover businesses near your location. Some fields may be missing if providers do not share them publicly.

Can I change the search area?

Yes. Pan or zoom the map and click “Search this area.” You can also type a city, address, or ZIP and press Enter.

Why do ratings or reviews look different from other sites?

Ratings and review counts come from Google users and may differ from other directories. We also let you lower filters to see more options.
